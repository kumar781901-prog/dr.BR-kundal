About Dr. B.R. Kundal

BR
MD Medicine
DM Neurology
Fellowship – Epilepsy
Member, IAN

Dedicated to the Health of Your Mind & Nervous System

Dr. B.R. Kundal is a highly experienced consultant neurologist with over two decades of practice in the diagnosis and management of complex neurological conditions. He completed his DM in Neurology from a premier medical institution and has since been committed to delivering world-class neurological care in Jammu & Kashmir.

His clinical interests span epilepsy, stroke, movement disorders, headache, and neuromuscular diseases. Dr. Kundal is known for his calm, empathetic communication style and his commitment to patient education — ensuring every patient fully understands their condition and treatment plan.

Beyond clinical practice, Dr. Kundal has contributed to regional neurology awareness programmes and actively participates in continuing medical education.
